About Us

Sri Venkateshwara Glass House, founded in 2019 by Mr. P. Velmurugan (M.B.A), is a trusted name in the glass and UPVC industry based in Karur. With a strong commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, we specialize in a wide range of services including UPVC windows and doors, aluminum and toughened glass partitions, ACP elevations, plywood and cupboard works, laminated and designer glass, and much more. Our work is powered by premium brands like Saint Gobain, Gold Plus, Gopal, Ozone, and Perfex to ensure that every project reflects durability and elegance. We serve residential, commercial, and industrial clients with tailor-made solutions that blend aesthetics with functionality. Backed by years of experience, skilled craftsmanship, and a dedication to timely delivery, Sri Venkateshwara Glass House stands as a reliable partner for builders, architects, interior designers, and homeowners alike.

Whether you are constructing a new building or renovating a space, we are here to bring your vision to life — with glass that speaks style.
