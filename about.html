About Sri Vengadeswara Glass House

Sri Vengadeswara Glass House is a leading supplier of toughened glass, UPVC windows and doors, aluminum partition works, and hardware fittings across Tamil Nadu. Based in Karur, we proudly deliver quality solutions to residential, commercial, and industrial projects throughout Chennai, Coimbatore, Madurai, Salem, Trichy, and beyond.

With a focus on innovation and durability, we offer a range of products including designer glass, shower cubicles, mirror works, PVC doors, false ceilings, and interior wood panels. We are committed to quality, affordability, and timely service—making us a trusted name among architects, builders, and homeowners.

We are associated with premium brands such as Saint Gobain, Gold Plus, Ozone, and Alstrong, ensuring the best for our clients. Whether you're building a new space or renovating, Sri Vengadeswara Glass House is your one-stop solution for stylish and reliable materials.

Visit us at Thiru.Vi.Ka Road, Karur, or contact us at +91 97919 17872 for personalized service. We deliver to all cities in Tamil Nadu.
