About the Founder

Mr. P. Velmurugan (M.B.A) is the founder of Sri Venkateshwara Glass House. With a strong business background and a passion for quality, he started the company in 2019 to offer reliable and stylish glass, UPVC, and interior solutions. His commitment to trust, timely service, and customer satisfaction has made SVG a trusted name in Karur and beyond.

About the Brand

Sri Venkateshwara Glass House is more than just a service provider — it’s a brand built on trust, quality, and innovation. About Our Services

At Sri Venkateshwara Glass House, we offer a wide range of high-quality services tailored for homes, offices, and commercial spaces. Our expertise includes UPVC windows and doors, aluminum and toughened glass partitions, ACP and HPL elevations, laminated and designer glass, cupboards, plywood works, and customized hardware installations. Every service is executed with attention to detail, using top-grade materials from trusted brands. Whether it's a new construction or a renovation project, we deliver stylish, durable, and functional solutions that meet your needs with perfection.

About Us

Sri Venkateshwara Glass House, founded in 2019 by Mr. P. Velmurugan (M.B.A), is a trusted name in the glass and UPVC industry based in Karur. With a strong commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, we specialize in a wide range of services including UPVC windows and doors, aluminum and toughened glass partitions, ACP elevations, plywood and cupboard works, laminated and designer glass, and much more. Our work is powered by premium brands like Saint Gobain, Gold Plus, Gopal, Ozone, and Perfex to ensure that every project reflects durability and elegance. We serve residential, commercial, and industrial clients with tailor-made solutions that blend aesthetics with functionality. Backed by years of experience, skilled craftsmanship, and a dedication to timely delivery, Sri Venkateshwara Glass House stands as a reliable partner for builders, architects, interior designers, and homeowners alike.
